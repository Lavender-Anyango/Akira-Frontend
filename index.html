My First Week experience at Akirachix

Applying to Akirachix is one of the best decisions I have ever made.
After four exciting days at AkiraChix, I've been able to immerse myself
in the dynamic learning environment, thanks to the supportive staff and alumni who
helped me catch up on the curriculum.The program is challenging but rewarding, and the
sense of community is strong. The food, cleanliness, and professionalism are top-notch,
creating an ideal environment for learning. If you're considering joining the codeHive program,
trust me, it's a step towards a fulfilling career in tech.

THE GOOD THINGS

  • The School is clean
  • Everyone is very friendly
  • The food is nice
  • The rules set up instills discipline and accountability

THE NOT SO GOOD THINGS

  1. There are no extracuriculum activities
